About me

My name is Jenny Pavlova and I am a young and passionate web developer from Sofia, Bulgaria. Most of my exerience comes from frontend development with AngularJS, jQuery, Twitter Bootstrap, HTML5, CSS3. I have also worked with various backend tech stacks including LAMP, MEAN and end-to-end Wordpress solutions.

I mostly tend to work remote from my home/local co-working spaces, however I was a backend developer in a local web development studio, where I learned a lot about collaboration and taking responsibilities.

I have some expirance with enterpreneurship - some time ago along with several friends we co-founded YCharge - a startup in the electic vehicle sector. We started off as a Starup Weekend project and won our place in the Start-It-Smart Pre-Accelerator's second batch.
